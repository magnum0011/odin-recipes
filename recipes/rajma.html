Rajma

Description

A delicious red kidney beans curry cooked in onions, tomatoes and basic spices.

Ingredients

  • Red Kidney Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Corriander Leaves
  • Spices
  • Salt to taste
  1. Boil Red Kidney Beans.
  2. Chop up Onions and Tomatoes.
  3. Heat up oil in a cooker and saute the chopped onions and tomatoes.
  4. Once done now add Spices and Salt in the cooker.
  5. Now add water to the cooker and add the boiled Kidney Beans to it. Close the pressure cooker lid.
  6. Cook until 3 Whistles.
  7. Now server hot with garnishing and Enjoy your meal :).
